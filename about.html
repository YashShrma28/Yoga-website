About

Yoga With Neetu is an online yoga platform created to help people improve their health, flexibility, and inner balance through the practice of yoga. The classes are guided by Neetu, a National Yoga Player with more than 5 years of experience in yoga practice and training. Through years of dedication and continuous practice, Neetu has developed a deep under standing of yoga techniques, posture alignment, and breathing practices. Her goal is to make yoga accessible and beneficial for everyone, whether they are beginners or experienced practitioners. Yoga With Neetu has also built a strong and supportive community online, with more than 100K followers on Instagram who regularly engage with yoga tips, wellness guidance, and practice sessions. This growing community reflects the trust and positive results that students experience through the classes. All sessions are conducted online, making it easy for students to practice yoga from the comfort of their homes. Each class focuses on improving flexibility, building body strength, increasing focus, and reducing daily stress through guided yoga poses and breathing techniques. The mission of Yoga With Neetu is to help people live healthier, calmer, and more balanced lives through the regular practice of yoga.
